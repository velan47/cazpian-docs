We are excited to announce **Cazpian**, a new kind of data platform built from the ground up for AWS. 

In today's world, data teams face a constant struggle: how to manage massive amounts of data without getting bogged down by infrastructure complexity. Cazpian solves this by combining the power of **Apache Iceberg** and **Apache Spark** into a seamless, managed experience.

<!-- truncate -->

### Why Cazpian?
Most platforms try to do everything. We focus on one thing: making your AWS data lake work like a high-performance warehouse. With our Lakehouse architecture, you get:
- **ACID Transactions**: Never worry about partial writes again.
- **Time Travel**: Query previous versions of your data effortlessly.
- **Schema Evolution**: Change your data structure without breaking downstream pipelines.
